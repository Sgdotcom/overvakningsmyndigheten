Demo

This site is an educational demo about passive data collection and fingerprinting.

What data is collected

  • Signals your browser can expose (device, network, storage, behavior) when you accept cookies / start the demo.
  • Some signals are optional and may require permissions (e.g. geolocation).

Storage & retention

  • The demo is designed to be client-side. Data is shown in your session and stored locally only for demonstration (cookies/localStorage/sessionStorage).
  • You can remove local traces with Purge all data in the demo. Note: HSTS is browser-managed and may require clearing site data/HSTS settings to fully reset.

No warranties

This is an art/education project. It may be inaccurate or incomplete, and some techniques may be blocked by modern browsers.
